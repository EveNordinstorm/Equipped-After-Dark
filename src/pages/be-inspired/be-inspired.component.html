The Process

Interview with Juliet Hammond

In conversation with our Head of Design and founder of Equipped After Dark.

"

Equipped After Dark was created to fill my own need of wanting practical clothing, fit for the UK weather, which also incorporates the latest tech-wear features to make it extra wearable. But most importantly, clothing that makes you look amazing and something you could wear on a night out that doesn't make you look like you're going hiking.

"

Hand Designed

My brand is all about quality. That's why we hand illustrate our designs before employing the latest digital and manufacturing technology.

Before Dawn Trainers

Background

I've always had a passion for design from a young age, even if I didn't realise that it was my designer's eye I was working on. I've always taken a lot of inspiration from my surroundings, whether that's architecture, what people wear or how people choose to decorate their homes.

Studying design more seriously, I feel like I've built this internal library of design genres. I know what's visually appealing to myself, but what has served me well is knowing what fits other people depending on their character and lifestyle.

Inspiration

I want to inspire the people who are visionaries, the ones who know who they want to be but maybe don't know exactly what that looks like yet. I want you to be able to express yourself through my designs and make them your own.

I'm constantly having ideas, attempting to bring them to life, inevitably experiencing failures but most importantly continuously developing as a creative. I urge you to do the same as your potential reflects in the dark.
